Privacy Policy

QueryVault by JASS Enterprises
Effective Date: February 18, 2026 Last Updated: March 9, 2026
QueryVault ("the Extension"), developed and operated by JASS Enterprises, is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy describes what information the Extension collects, how it is used, and your rights regarding that information. This policy applies to all versions of the Extension distributed through the Chrome Web Store, Microsoft Edge Add-ons, and any other browser extension marketplace.

1. Information We Collect

A. All Users (Community Edition)

The Extension stores the following data locally in your browser:

  • Saved Keepa Product Finder query configurations (form field names and values)
  • Query metadata (names, creation/update timestamps, favorite status)
  • Extension preferences (UI preferences)

B. Registered Users (QueryVault+)

When you choose to register in-app to unlock additional features (30 saved queries, export/import/merge), we collect:

  • Your name
  • Your email address

Registration is entirely voluntary. By submitting your name and email, you consent to JASS Enterprises collecting and using this information for the purposes described in Section 2 below. This information is transmitted securely (via HTTPS) and stored on JASS Enterprises systems.

C. What We Do NOT Collect

The Extension does NOT collect, store, or transmit:

  • Your saved query data — queries always remain local in your browser
  • Browsing history, cookies, or activity on any website
  • Analytics, telemetry, or usage tracking data

2. How We Use Your Information

Query data (stored locally) is used exclusively to:

  • Store your Keepa Product Finder search configurations
  • Restore saved configurations when you load a query
  • Sync your favorite query references across devices (via Chrome Sync)

Registration data (name and email, registered users only) may be used to:

  • Validate your registered status and unlock QueryVault+ features
  • Send you product updates, new feature announcements, and information about related JASS Enterprises products and services
  • Notify you of important changes to the Extension or this privacy policy

We will NEVER sell, rent, lease, or share your name, email address, or any contact details with any third party — for any reason, under any circumstances. Your registration information is used exclusively by JASS Enterprises to communicate with you about our own products and services.

3. Data Storage and Security

Query data is stored locally in your browser using Chrome's built-in Extension Storage API (chrome.storage.local and chrome.storage.sync). Your saved queries are never sent to any external server.

Registration data (name and email for QueryVault+ users) is transmitted securely via HTTPS and stored on JASS Enterprises systems. We implement reasonable administrative and technical safeguards to protect this information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or loss.

Chrome Sync data (favorite query references only — not full query data) is handled by Google's Chrome Sync infrastructure and is subject to Google's privacy practices.

The Extension only makes network requests when a user explicitly initiates registration. No background network activity, no silent data transmission, and no telemetry pings occur at any time.

4. Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, trade, rent, or share your personal information with any third party. This includes your name, email address, and any data associated with your use of the Extension. No exceptions.

The Extension has no third-party analytics services, no advertising networks, and no data brokers. Your registration information is held solely by JASS Enterprises and is never disclosed to outside parties.

5. Your Rights and Control

You have full control over your data:

  • View all saved queries in the Extension's side panel
  • Delete any or all saved queries at any time
  • Uninstall the Extension to remove all locally stored data
  • Request deletion of your registration information (name and email) at any time by contacting us at the address in Section 10 — we will remove your information promptly
  • Opt out of product communications at any time by contacting us or using the unsubscribe mechanism in any email we send

6. Data Retention

Query data persists in your browser until you delete it or uninstall the Extension. It is never stored on our servers.

Registration data (name and email) is retained by JASS Enterprises for as long as your QueryVault+ registration remains active, or until you request deletion. To request deletion, contact us at the address in Section 10 and we will remove your information within 30 days.

7. Children's Privacy

The Extension does not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

8. Browser Permissions

The Extension requests the following browser permissions, each used solely for its stated purpose:

  • Storage / Unlimited Storage — to save your query configurations locally in your browser
  • Side Panel — to display the QueryVault interface alongside Keepa
  • Active Tab / Scripting — to read and restore form field values on the Keepa Product Finder page
  • Host permission (keepa.com) — to interact with Keepa's Product Finder forms only

No permissions are used to access data on any website other than keepa.com. No permissions are used for advertising, analytics, or tracking.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date above. Continued use of the Extension after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact
